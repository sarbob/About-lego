Some cool facts about lego

  1. The total number of minifigures produced could fill about 170 standard-sized swimming pools!
  2. About 12 minifigures are produced every second!
  3. You can combine six of the eight-studded LEGO bricks in 915,103,765 ways!
  4. The plural of Lego is “Lego.”
  5. The first Lego bricks were made entirely of wood.
  6. There are more than 400 billion Lego bricks in the world!
  7. If all the Lego bricks in the world were stacked on top of one another, they would create a structure nearly 2.4 million miles tall, which is 10 times the distance to the moon!
  8. The amount of Lego bricks sold in just one year could wrap around the Earth five times.
  9. Around the world, seven Lego sets are sold every second. That’s more than 600,000 every day!
